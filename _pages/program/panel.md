## Monday, July 6, 2026 Session: Careers in NLP - 9:00 - 10:00 AM - Balboa

### Panel: Careers in NLP

### Panelists

![Roberto Navigli](/assets/images/speakers/Roberto_Navigli.jpg){: .align-center .speaker-photo}
Roberto Navigli <br>
Professor, Sapienza University of Rome & Scientific Director, Babelscape
{: .speaker-info}

[Roberto Navigli](https://www.diag.uniroma1.it/navigli) is Professor of Natural Language Processing at Sapienza University of Rome, where he leads the [Sapienza NLP Group](http://nlp.uniroma1.it/). He is a Fellow of ACL, AAAI, ELLIS, and EurAI. He has received two ERC grants on multilingual lexical and sentence-level semantics, highlighted among the 15 projects [through which the ERC has transformed science](https://erc.europa.eu/how-ERC-transformed-science/), as well as several prizes and awards from leading AI and NLP journals and conferences. He is the creator of [BabelNet](http://babelnet.org/) and leads the [Minerva LLM and ChatMinerva projects](https://minerva-ai.org/) - the first family of LLMs and AI assistants pretrained with a focus on Italian, and among the very few initiatives of this kind in Europe. He is also Scientific Director and co-founder of [Babelscape](https://babelscape.com/), a successful deep-tech company with ten years of experience in research-driven multilingual neuro-symbolic NLP. He is an Action Editor of TACL, served as General Chair of ACL 2025, and has served as an Associate Editor of the AI Journal.
{: .speaker-bio}

![Trung Bui](/assets/images/committee/Trung_Bui.jpg){: .align-center .speaker-photo}
Trung Bui <br>
Senior Research Scientist and Research Manager - Adobe Research
{: .speaker-info}

Trung is a researcher and manager of the Natural Language Group at Adobe Research, where he develops machine learning technologies for Adobe digital media and digital marketing products, spanning dialogue systems, multimodal interfaces, search and recommendation, and AI for healthcare. His honors include the SIGDIAL 2018 Best Paper Award, first place in the 2016 Dialog State Tracking Challenge, and the 2021 ACM SIGAI Industry Award for Excellence in Artificial Intelligence for healthcare AI work he co-led. Before joining Adobe Research in 2012, Trung held research roles at EPFL, the University of Twente, Stanford University, and North Side Inc., a path spanning academia and industry across four countries. Since 2004, he has authored more than 100 peer-reviewed publications and holds over 60 patents, with more than 60 pending. He has mentored or co-mentored roughly 80 interns and serves as an area chair for the ACL Industry Track, NeurIPS, and ICML.
{: .speaker-bio}

### Moderator

![Yang Liu](/assets/images/committee/Yang_Liu.jpeg){: .align-center .speaker-photo}
Yang Liu <br>
Senior Principal Scientist, AGI, Amazon
{: .speaker-info}

Yang Liu is a Senior Principal Scientist at Amazon AGI. Before joining Amazon, she served as Head of the LAIX Silicon Valley AI Lab, Research Scientist at Facebook, Visiting Scientist at Google, faculty member at the University of Texas at Dallas, and researcher at ICSI, Berkeley. Yang received her B.S. and M.S. from Tsinghua University and her Ph.D. from Purdue University. As a faculty member at UT Dallas, she received the NSF CAREER Award and the Air Force Young Investigator (YIP) Award. She served as General Chair for ACL 2023 and Program Co-Chair for EMNLP 2020. She is an IEEE Fellow and an ISCA Fellow.
{: .speaker-bio}
