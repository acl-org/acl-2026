We are excited to invite you to the ACL 2026 Social Event, taking place aboard one of San Diego’s most iconic landmarks—the historic USS Midway.

**Venue:** USS Midway Museum (910 N Harbor Dr, San Diego, CA 92101)
**Location:** A short walk from the Manchester Grand Hyatt along the waterfront
**Date & Time:** Monday, July 6, 2026, 18:30–22:00

Step aboard this legendary aircraft carrier—once the longest-serving U.S. Navy aircraft carrier of the 20th century—for an unforgettable evening of networking, exploration, and fun. Now a world-renowned floating museum, the USS Midway offers a unique opportunity to experience history firsthand while connecting with colleagues from across the ACL community.

#### What to Expect On Board:
 - Explore the expansive flight deck featuring restored aircraft, helicopters, and fighter jets
 - Try out interactive flight simulators and hands-on exhibits
 - Enjoy breathtaking views of San Diego Bay at sunset and into the evening
 - Walk through authentic crew quarters, engine rooms, and command centers
 - Engage with docents and exhibits sharing real stories of life at sea

#### Fun Activities & Experiences:
 - Themed networking spaces across multiple decks
 - Music and entertainment in a truly unique historic setting
 - Food and drinks served throughout the evening
 - Plenty of opportunities for photos on the flight deck and around the ship

With over 60 exhibits and a massive multi-level layout, the USS Midway provides a one-of-a-kind venue blending history, innovation, and community.

We recommend wearing comfortable shoes as you explore the ship’s many levels and spaces.

We look forward to welcoming you aboard for an unforgettable evening!

#### Important Information:
 - Attendance is included for all Main Conference registrants.
 - If you are not registered for the Main Conference but wish to attend, you may add this event at the Registration Solutions Desk, located on the lobby level.

Don’t forget: the Oral/Poster/Demo Sessions begin at 9:00 AM the following morning.
